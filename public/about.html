About HomeProHub

Our Mission

HomeProHub is revolutionizing how homeowners connect with contractors through the power of artificial intelligence. Our mission is to make home improvement transparent, efficient, and stress-free for everyone involved.

We believe that finding a trustworthy contractor shouldn't be a gamble. That's why we've built an intelligent platform that combines AI-powered diagnostics with verified contractor bidding to give homeowners confidence in their hiring decisions.

10k+ Projects Completed
5k+ Verified Contractors
98% Satisfaction Rate

How We're Different

AI-Powered Diagnosis: Our proprietary AI analyzes photos and descriptions of home issues to provide instant professional insights about severity, urgency, and estimated costs.

Transparent Bidding: Homeowners post once and receive multiple bids from verified contractors. Compare prices, timelines, and credentials side-by-side.

Two-Way Ratings: Both homeowners and contractors rate each other, creating accountability and trust on both sides of the marketplace.

Verified Professionals: All contractors undergo license verification, insurance checks, and background screening before joining our platform.

Our Team

HomeProHub was founded by a team of technologists and home improvement professionals who experienced firsthand the challenges of finding reliable contractors. We're passionate about using technology to solve real-world problems and creating value for homeowners and contractors alike.

Our team combines expertise in artificial intelligence, marketplace platforms, and the construction industry to build a product that truly serves both sides of the market.
