Help Center

For Homeowners

How does the AI diagnosis work?
Upload photos of your issue and describe what's happening. Our AI analyzes the images and text to identify the problem, assess severity and urgency, and provide estimated cost ranges based on thousands of similar projects.
How do I post a job?
After creating an account, use our AI Assistant to describe your issue. Once analyzed, you can post it as a job with one click. Local contractors will receive your posting and submit bids.
How many bids will I receive?
You typically receive 3-10 bids within 24-48 hours, depending on your location and the type of work. You're never obligated to accept any bid.
Are contractors verified?
Yes. All contractors go through license verification, insurance checks, and background screening. We also require valid business licenses where applicable.
Is HomeProHub free for homeowners?
Yes! Creating an account, using the AI assistant, and receiving bids is completely free. There are no hidden fees or commissions.

For Contractors

How do I sign up as a contractor?
Click "Get Started" and select "Contractor" as your role. You'll need to provide your business name, license information, and insurance details for verification.
How much does it cost?
Creating a profile and browsing jobs is free. We offer premium features and lead credits for contractors who want priority placement and additional job opportunities.
How do I submit a bid?
Browse the job board, click on jobs that match your expertise, review the AI diagnostic report, and submit your bid with price range, timeline, and a personalized message.
Can I message homeowners directly?
Yes. Once you submit a bid, you can message the homeowner through our secure platform to discuss project details, ask questions, and finalize agreements.
How do reviews work?
After completing a project, both you and the homeowner can leave reviews. Verified reviews help build your reputation and win more jobs.
